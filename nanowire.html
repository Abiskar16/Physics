Metal Nitride Nanowire Growth on Si(110)

This project focuses on the growth of transition metal nitride nanostructures on silicon substrates using molecular beam epitaxy.

Project Overview

The main goal is to study how growth conditions such as substrate temperature, nitrogen plasma, metal flux, and surface reconstruction affect nanowire formation.

Methods

  • Molecular Beam Epitaxy growth
  • RHEED monitoring during growth
  • SEM surface morphology analysis
  • XPS chemical state analysis
  • XRD and AFM characterization

Current Status

Nanostructures were observed on Si(110). Further analysis is being done to understand growth direction, morphology, and crystallinity.
