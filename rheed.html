RHEED Study of Silicon Surface Reconstruction

This project focuses on monitoring silicon surface reconstruction and thin film growth behavior using reflection high-energy electron diffraction.

Project Overview

RHEED patterns are used to understand surface crystallinity, reconstruction, roughness, and growth mode during annealing and deposition.

Topics

  • Si surface reconstruction
  • RHEED streaks and spots
  • Growth mode monitoring
  • Temperature-dependent surface changes
